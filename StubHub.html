PRODUCT DESIGN INTERN

STUBHUB

Time - Fall 2020

Role - Product Design Intern

Colleagues - Design Managers, UX Designers, Product Managers, and various Developer Teams

During my 3 months at StubHub, I had the opportunity to wear multiple hats and work on different projects as an intern. The projects I worked on range from ticket delivery, system messaging, to designing internal tools.

#1 - Service Blueprints

Collaborating with my Design Manager and Product Managers, I produced 5 service blueprints that captured StubHub’s ticket delivery & fulfillment services, from frontend experiences to backend processes. 2 to 3 actionable painpoints were discovered for each type of delivery method.

StubHub's Overall Service(blurred for confidentiality)

The Problem

Delivering the tickets from sellers to buyers is one of the core stages of StubHub’s services. However, there are a LOT of delivery methods for live events, and such variety makes the delivery & fulfillment process innately complex. By making service blueprints, we aimed to lay out the major delivery methods in detail and pinpoint potential pain points in the delivery process.

Service Blueprints for StubHub's four most popular ticket medium(blurred for confidentiality)

What I Learned

A lot of times, the problems that affect user experiences reside in the backend processes. As a Product Designer, it is my responsibility to dig into the root of problems. For this project, I spent a lot of time communicating with many different development teams and trying to make sense of complex backend processes. At the end of this project, I realized the technical ambiguities in large-scale services and managed to identify solvable problems within ambiguity.

#2 - Designing Internal Platforms

Working with Tech Leads from Dev Teams, I designed the experiences and interfaces of 2 new features for StubHub’s internal Trust&Security platform.

Wireframes for StubHub's Internal Web Platforms

Accelerating Task Flows for Internal Users

Users of these internal tools are mostly people from other departments of the company. When designing for these stakeholders, the essential job for me was to understand how they navigate their tasks as of now. For this project, I communicated heavily with the internal users and made sure the experience I designed not only aligned, but also accelerated their current task flow.

What I Learned

When it comes to internal platforms, Efficiency is Key. In this project, I focused on and learned how to utilize data-visualizations efficiently as an element that helps display and manage data faster.

#3 - Messaging System

In this project, I worked with my mentor designer and Product Managers to iterate and improve StubHub’s messaging system. I gathered useful insights through competitor analysis on StubHub’s direct and indirect competitors and am designing new messaging templates for StubHub’s email messaging system.

This project is currently under work. Contact me for details :).
